## 📌 Project summary

This dashboard analyses sales performance for **Cookie Bliss** — a fictional cookie manufacturer selling to retailers across the UK, France, and Italy. It covers two datasets: a practice dataset (10 Chocolate Chip orders across 5 retailers) and a full orders dataset (150+ orders with revenue, cost, and shipment data).

**Dataset covers:**
- Orders with revenue, cost, and profit data
- Rush vs standard shipment breakdown
- Retailer performance across 3 countries (UK, France, Italy)
- Multiple customer segments

---

## 🔍 Key insights

- **Customer 325698 is the highest-volume buyer** — the most frequent purchaser in the dataset, appearing in over 20 transactions.
- **Rush shipments make up a significant share of orders** — indicating strong demand urgency from key customers.
- **Revenue consistently runs at 2× cost** — reflecting a stable 50% profit margin built into the pricing model.
- **UK-based retailers lead in order volume** — ChocoWorld and Cookie Heaven appear more frequently in high-quantity orders than French or Italian counterparts.
